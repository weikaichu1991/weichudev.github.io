Life Outside of Dev

This part of the website which I call it a Journal documents my passion of photogrpahy to art, food, history and architecture outside of the professional life. I always take a lot of photos whenever I go out for traveling, even just a night out in London where I live. The experiences is just getting a bit too rich and the moments are just too beautiful to not sharing them with my own words to the people around me. Therefore, here I share my eyes, my senses, and my memory with you. Hoping you will enjoy looking through them.
